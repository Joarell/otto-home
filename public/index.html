Solving the problem.

Let's provide a little context.
We all know that it is quite normal for you to get the estimate approved; however, some changes might take a long time. On average between these updates takes around 2 days to calculate all costs before reverting them to clients.

Despite that, the caveat in these situations is the number of works on the list and how complex the works are in terms of design new crates. Therefore, in most cases, it can reduce the chances of getting approval by not replaying at the right time.

By using Otto, you will be able to pay more attention to your costumer and provide efficiency for them!
Even when you do not have an INTERNET CONNECTION!

The Otto Crate Solver has been created to reduce your estimated lead time as quickly as possible.

It can handle five types of work crates:



  • Tubes;
  • Same size canvas;
  • Sculptures and furniture;
  • Huge canvas;
  • Mix size canvas;

Almost all crates are target to PAX flights, which have much more favorable shipping rates,
assuming the average plane's door size is:



  • 300 x 200 x 160 - Centimeters;
  • 118.11 x 78.74 x 62.99 - Inches;
